What is a Learning Managment System?
A Learning Management System (LMS) is a tool that houses all of the learning content and collateral at RMN. Everything from course sign ups and descriptions to pre-work and assignments to e-learning opportunities will live in our LMS.
How do I find a course?
You can search our full catalog of courses using the Search Page. You can search by course name, topic, area of interest etc.

How do I enroll in a Lynda.com course?
Each Lynda.com course listed in the system will direct you to a Google Form to sign up for a Lynda.com license. RMNU has a set number of licenses that we assign to team members each month. By signing up on the Google Form, you place yourself on the waitlist for a Lynda.com license the next month that a license is available.

How do I add my courses to my G-cal?
For each live training RMNU offers, you will receive an email notification from our LMS. In this email, you will recieve a link that, when clicked, will add the training to your G-cal.
